Sustainability

🌍 SDG Goals Alignment

This project supports sustainable agriculture and aligns with several United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). By enabling smarter, resource-efficient mushroom cultivation, the system contributes to food security, environmental responsibility, and technological innovation.

  • 🌾

    Zero Hunger

    Improving mushroom cultivation efficiency helps increase food production and supports sustainable, local food supply chains.

  • 🏗

    Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

    Applying IoT technology to agriculture demonstrates how digital infrastructure can modernize traditional farming practices.

  • ♻

    Responsible Consumption & Production

    Automated monitoring reduces waste by maintaining optimal growing conditions, minimizing failed harvests and resource overuse.

  • 🌍

    Climate Action

    Mushrooms have a low environmental footprint. Optimizing their cultivation with smart monitoring further reduces the carbon cost of food production.
